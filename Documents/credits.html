What It Is

In case you were wondering, the character depicted on the Welcome Screen is a three-tailed Nogitsune. To those of us affiliated with Furry Fandom, it is known as a "Fursona" -- a type of alter ego/imaginary friend/on-line role-play character/subject of fan-pics and/or fan-fics, etc.
